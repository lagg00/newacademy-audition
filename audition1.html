Eligibility & Age

Please only proceed if your applicant is 12 to 14 years old. Birth year must be selected. We will validate age automatically.

Training & Commitment

Training will include intense weekend sessions. This project expects commitment for 1–2 years of training, with potential debut timelines of 5–7 years depending on development. Parents must be comfortable with this schedule.

Expectations

There is no enforced strict diet, but there will be consistent, sometimes intense exercise and practice. Popularity at debut is not guaranteed; you may build a following on social media before and during debut.

Project Nature

New Academy is a Christian-based group project initiated by a single creator; it is not company-owned. Everyone is treated equally — there is no single leader. This is an independent, faith-centered creative project.

Content & Privacy

All submission materials are used for audition review only. Do not submit copyrighted performance content you do not own permission for. Your contact info will not be publicly posted.
