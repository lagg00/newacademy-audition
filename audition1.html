Terms & Service — Please read

Important — Read carefully:

  • New Academy is a Christian-based audition program focused on ministry and spreading the gospel through music. This is not a professional label; progress may be slow and we ask for patience.
  • No strict dieting policies — we will not require unhealthy or extreme diets. Health and safety come first.
  • Ages allowed: 12–14 only. Parent/guardian permission is required for anyone under 18.
  • Training can be intense. Be prepared for regular, sometimes long, practice sessions.
  • Commitment: training may last multiple years; we expect members (with parental support) to commit to training for a sustained period.
  • We do not encourage joining solely for fame or money. The group’s mission is ministry and sharing faith through performance.
  • Team culture: there is no single 'leader'. Members must be respectful, social, and report problems to parents/staff.
  • If you accept, parents must support and communicate with staff as needed.
